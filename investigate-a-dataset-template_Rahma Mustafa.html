Introduction¶

In this project we will be analyzing FBI gun data, the FBI National Instant Criminal Background Check System provide this data to us and it in range 1998 to 2017 . The data have two tables:

First dataset is called gun data os in .xlsx extention, it explains the type of guns that premit and registered, the states and the date that permit in.

Second dataset is called U.S census data is in .csv extention, it explains some Facts like who buy and sell guns in states, this dataset is limited, so we will extract little information from it.

we will ask some research questions and after gathering and filterd datasets, the questions had to be answered

Our Research Questions :¶

First question: Which highest state in permit?

Second question: Which the highest state that the Persons 65 years and over, percent, April 1, 2010 is?

Third question: Which month in the year have highest permit in guns?

Fourth question: What the average of the all firms in 2012 for all states?

Fifth question: What is the type of gun that have high totals in all states?

Sixth question: What is the total of guns for all states and what the overall for gun growth?
